# MAS Financial Planning Guide — Key Benchmarks

Source: MAS and Financial Industry Basic Financial Planning Guide (2023)

## Life Insurance (Death Benefit)

For working adults with dependents, MAS recommends life insurance coverage of **9 to 10 times annual income**. This accounts for income replacement, outstanding debts (mortgage, loans), and dependents' future expenses.

- Recommended multiplier: 9–10x annual income
- Category: life
- Value: 9.0
- Value high: 10.0
- Value type: income_multiplier
- Source page: 12

## Total Permanent Disability (TPD)

TPD coverage should be equivalent to life coverage as it replaces the earning capacity permanently lost due to disability.

- Recommended multiplier: 9–10x annual income (same as life)
- Category: tpd
- Value: 9.0
- Value high: 10.0
- Value type: income_multiplier
- Source page: 13

## Disability Income

MAS recommends disability income insurance that replaces **75% of monthly income** to cover living expenses if you are unable to work due to illness or injury. Benefits typically pay until recovery or age 65.

- Recommended replacement: 75% of monthly income
- Category: disability
- Value: 0.75
- Value type: percentage
- Source page: 14

## Savings and Emergency Fund

MAS recommends maintaining an emergency fund of 3–6 months of monthly expenses before purchasing additional insurance products.

- Source page: 8

## Premium Burden

MAS recommends that total insurance premiums should not exceed **15% of annual income** to avoid financial strain.

- Source page: 15
