# MediShield Life Benefits — Overview

Source: Ministry of Health Singapore, MediShield Life Benefits page

## What is MediShield Life?

MediShield Life is Singapore's basic health insurance plan, automatically covering all Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents. It helps pay for large hospitalisation bills and selected costly outpatient treatments.

**Key point:** EP holders and foreigners are NOT enrolled in MediShield Life and must purchase their own hospitalisation insurance.

## MediShield Life Coverage

MediShield Life covers:
- Hospitalisation in Class B2/C wards at public hospitals
- Daily ward charges up to SGD 800/day (before co-insurance and deductibles)
- ICU charges up to SGD 2,000/day
- Selected outpatient treatments (chemotherapy, dialysis, etc.)

## Deductibles and Co-insurance

- Annual deductible: SGD 1,500–3,000 (age-based, waived for B2/C ward)
- Co-insurance: 10% of remaining bill after deductible
- MediSave can be used to pay deductible and co-insurance

## CareShield Life

CareShield Life provides long-term care insurance for severe disability (unable to perform 3 or more Activities of Daily Living). All Singapore Citizens and PRs born in 1980 or later are automatically enrolled.

- Monthly payout starts at SGD 600 (2024), increasing 2% annually
- Payable for as long as severely disabled
- Category: careshield
- Value type: boolean
- Source page: careshield-section

**CareShield Life Supplement recommendation:** Those enrolled in CareShield Life should consider a supplement policy to top up the monthly payout to cover actual disability care costs, which can exceed SGD 3,000/month.

## Integrated Shield Plan (IP) Recommendation

For residents who want better coverage than MediShield Life's B2/C ward default, an Integrated Shield Plan (IP) is available from private insurers. The IP upgrades your coverage to a higher ward class.
